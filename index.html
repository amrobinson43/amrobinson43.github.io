Andrew Robinson, PhD

I am a climatologist specializing in heat stress, urban microclimates, and environmental modeling. My work focuses on high-resolution mapping of Wet Bulb Globe Temperature, land-surface controls on heat exposure, and applied climate research that connects environmental data to real-world risk.
